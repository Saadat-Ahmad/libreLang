Mission

مقصد

Our mission is to make Urdu learning enjoyable and widely accessible by providing a complete, high-quality curriculum that anyone can access from anywhere with ease. We aim to make learning Urdu fun and easy for everyone. We do not want to see such rich cultural heritage slowly die out or worse appropriated by foreign powers.

Vision

نظریہ

We envision a world where Urdu can be learned freely through immersive, beautifully designed digital lessons. We want to make the new generation of Urdu learners feel proud of their language and culture.

About us

ہمارے بارے میں

LibreUrdu is an open-source project that offers structured lessons, vocabulary practice, reading material, and interactive tools that help beginners and advanced learners improve their Urdu fluency. We are a team of volunteers who are passionate about Urdu and want to make it accessible to everyone.
